About Citrus Heights

Citrus Heights was founded in 2022 by a team of AI researchers, sales professionals, and software engineers who recognized a critical gap in the market. Traditional chatbots felt robotic and ineffective, while manual lead generation was consuming valuable time and resources. We set out to build something different—an AI chatbot engine powered by Claude that could have genuinely intelligent conversations, qualify leads accurately, and integrate seamlessly with existing business systems. Today, Citrus Heights powers lead generation for hundreds of companies across industries, from startups to Fortune 500 enterprises.

Our mission is simple: to democratize AI-powered lead generation so that every business, regardless of size, can compete at the highest level. We believe that artificial intelligence should amplify human potential, not replace it. That's why we've built Citrus Heights to work alongside your team, handling the repetitive work of lead qualification and follow-up while your sales professionals focus on building relationships and closing deals. We're committed to continuous innovation, transparent pricing, and customer success—because your growth is our growth.
